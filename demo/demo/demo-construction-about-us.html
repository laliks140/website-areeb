Unique and Quality Services

What We Do Best

PINEMACHINERY is a company specialized in worldwide trade in new and used construction machinery, trucks, cranes, and parts.

At our location in Valencia (ES), we have a large stock of high quality construction equipment, trucks, and cranes. On this location, we have a big testing area where interested customers can test and try the desired machine. Our most popular brands are Bobcat, Caterpillar and Volvo. Besides those brands, we also have other well-known brands in stock like Komatsu, Liebherr, JCB, Tadano, Grove etc. At our location we also have a large stock of used parts for the most common types of construction machines. These parts are dismantled by our own mechanics and have all been checked and/or rebuilt. Besides this, we also supply every desired part new, as original or after-market. Our workshop is equipped with the newest tools and diagnostic equipment to repair any machine. PINEMACHINERY specialises in the worldwide shipping of all sold machines. Sold parts can be quickly shipped or sent by land, sea or air freight. You are most welcome to visit us. Please make an appointment.
